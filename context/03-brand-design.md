- **Display/Headlines:** Gloock (serif, editorial)

- **Mono/Labels:** Space Mono (technical accents)

## Visual Rules (DO)
- Generous whitespace — Anthropic-inspired editorial warmth
- Soft gradients, nothing aggressive
- Dark hero bg (#191924) → light cream body (#FAF9F7)
- Pillar colors used contextually (Data section = lavender tones, etc.)
- Animations: soft, purposeful, calming — "a gentle exhale"

## Visual Rules (DON'T)
- No bento grid layouts
- No neon / cyberpunk / glassmorphism
- No aggressive animations or motion sickness
- No generic stock-photo AI imagery (robot arms, floating brains)

## Motion / Animation
- Hover: 150–200ms ease-out
- Page transitions: 300–400ms cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)
- Scroll reveals: 500–700ms
- "Animations feel like a gentle exhale"

## Logo System
- 3×3 dot matrix — data, connectivity, neural pathways
- Three highlighted dots form a diagonal (zero to one journey)
- Color-coded letters: **D** (Lavender) **·** **O** (Mint) **·** **T** (Peach) **·** **S** (Sky)
- Files: `public/*.svg`, `public/*.png` (various sizes)

## Tone of Voice

### Do
- Lead with outcomes, not features
- Plain language — "10 year old test"
- Back claims with specific numbers ($21.75 in 1.8s)
- AI feels approachable, not intimidating
- Confident without arrogant

### Don't
- No buzzwords ("leverage," "synergy," "paradigm shift")
- No enterprise jargon ("vertically-integrated," "multi-modal data streams")
- Never aggressive in sales — let the work speak
