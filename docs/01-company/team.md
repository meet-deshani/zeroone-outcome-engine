## Operating Model: 1 PM + Network of Agents

ZeroOne operates with a lean human team augmented by AI agents. The model is simple: one human Project Manager coordinates a network of specialized AI agents that handle operational, analytical, and technical work.

This isn't hypothetical — it's how we run our own company and how we build systems for clients.

---

## The AI Council (Our AI Team)

We don't rely on a single AI model. We run a council of specialized models, each with a role:

| Role | Model | What They Do |
|------|-------|-------------|
| **CEO** | Claude (Anthropic) | Deep reasoning, architecture decisions, complex planning, strategy |
| **CTO** | Gemini Pro (Google) | Code generation, technical review, balanced workloads |
| **COO** | Gemini Flash (Google) | Quick responses, summaries, routine tasks, high-volume ops |
| **Team** | DeepSeek R1 (Local) | Offline worker — runs on your server, zero cloud cost, no data leaves |

### How the Failover Works

If one model goes down, the system automatically routes to the next:

```
Claude (CEO) → Gemini Pro (CTO) → Gemini Flash (COO) → DeepSeek R1 (Local) → Always Answers
```

This means:
- **Zero downtime** — the system always responds
- **Best model for each task** — complex queries go to Claude, simple ones to Flash
- **Cost optimized** — mix premium cloud models with free local models
- **Data stays private** — local DeepSeek handles anything that shouldn't leave your server

## How We Staff Client Projects

Every client engagement follows the same pattern:

1. **1 Human PM** — Your single point of contact. Understands your business, coordinates everything.
2. **AI Agent Team** — Specialized agents deployed for your specific use case (from our catalog of 12+ agents).
3. **AI Council Backbone** — The multi-model infrastructure powering all agents with automatic failover.

This means clients get enterprise-grade AI capabilities at a fraction of the traditional team cost. No benching 10 consultants — just 1 PM and a fleet of agents that never sleep.
