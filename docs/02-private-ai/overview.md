## The Problem: Renting AI Is Broken

Most businesses today "rent" AI from big tech companies. Here's what that means:

| What You Get | What It Costs |
|-------------|---------------|
| Access to a model (ChatGPT, Gemini, etc.) | Per-token pricing that scales up forever |
| Data sent to someone else's cloud | Loss of privacy and control |
| No ownership of outputs | Can't patent, can't customize, can't keep |
| Dependency on internet and uptime | When their servers go down, you go down |
| One model, one approach | No failover, no choice |

**The result:** You pay more every month, own nothing, and depend on someone else's infrastructure.

---

## Our Solution: Private AI You Own

ZeroOne builds AI systems that flip every one of those problems:

- **Runs on YOUR servers** — not our cloud, not Big Tech's cloud
- **Works without internet** — download once, use forever
- **100% yours** — file a patent, publish a research paper, it's your IP
- **Zero vendor lock-in** — walk away anytime with everything
- **Multiple models** — automatic failover means zero downtime

## The Three Pillars

Private AI stands on three pillars. Each one solves a different piece of the ownership puzzle:

### 1. AI Without Internet
> *Download once, use forever.*

On-device AI that works completely offline. Supports language models, speech-to-text, text-to-speech, and voice detection. Runs on phones, tablets, servers, and edge devices with GPU acceleration.

**Best for:** Healthcare clinics, manufacturing floors, field sales teams, any business with unreliable internet.

[Read full documentation →](./ai-without-internet.md)

### 2. AI Council
> *A team of AIs, not just one.*

A multi-model architecture where different AI models play different roles (CEO, CTO, COO). Automatic failover means if one model goes down, the next picks up instantly. Mixes premium cloud models with free local models for cost optimization.

**Best for:** Any business that needs reliable, always-on AI with cost control.

[Read full documentation →](./ai-council.md)

### 3. AI Office
> *9 tools on one server. Zero external dependencies.*

A complete AI infrastructure stack deployed as Docker containers on a single server. Includes chat, search, automation, databases, APIs, and security — all self-hosted.

**Best for:** Businesses that want a complete AI stack without managing 9 different vendors.

[Read full documentation →](./ai-office.md)
