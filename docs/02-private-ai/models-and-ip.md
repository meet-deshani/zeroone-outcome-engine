## Patent Rights

You can file a patent on your custom AI system. Here's what that means:

### What You Can Patent
- **Novel AI methods** you develop with our help (unique data processing, custom algorithms)
- **Industry-specific applications** (a new way to detect fraud in your sector, a unique quality control method)
- **Custom model architectures** if you develop novel neural network designs
- **Process innovations** — new ways AI improves your specific business operations

### What You Need
1. **Novelty** — your approach must be new (not previously published)
2. **Non-Obviousness** — it can't be a trivial application of existing tech
3. **Utility** — it must have a practical use

### ZeroOne's Role
- We build the system and transfer all IP to you
- We provide technical documentation suitable for patent applications
- We do not retain any rights to your custom models or methods
- We can provide expert technical descriptions for patent filings

## How Custom Models Are Built

1. **Discovery** — We understand your domain, your data, and your specific problem

4. **Fine-Tuning** — Train the model on your domain data using LoRA/QLoRA techniques
5. **Evaluation** — Benchmark against your quality standards
6. **Deployment** — Deploy on your server, integrate with your systems
7. **Transfer** — Hand over everything: code, model, data, docs, configs
