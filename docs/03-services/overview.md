**Services:** AI Agents (Data Guardians), Infinity CRM (Data Agent), Private AI

Machines do the boring work so your team doesn't have to. Automated reports, smart alerts, deadline tracking, and demand prediction.

Clear plans that turn data into growth. Live P&L modeling, waste finding, SEO optimization, AI visibility.
