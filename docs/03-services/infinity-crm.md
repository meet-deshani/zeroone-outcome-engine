## What It Is

Infinity CRM is an AI-first CRM layer that sits on top of your existing tools. It doesn't replace your CRM, helpdesk, or analytics — it connects them all and adds AI intelligence on top.

Think of it as a smart middleware: your existing stack on one side, your teams on the other, and AI in the middle making everything work better.

## 5 Core Agents

### 1. Infinity CRM Agent
- **Job:** Unified, action-ready view of every customer
- **What it does:** Pulls customer data from every system into one view. No switching between 5 tabs to understand a customer.
- **Output:** Single customer profile with full history, actions, and context

### 2. Data Agent
- **Job:** Connects to your databases, dashboards, and APIs
- **What it does:** Answers questions in plain English. "What was our churn last month?" — it queries, computes, and responds.
- **Output:** Instant data answers without SQL or analyst bottlenecks

### 3. Ops Agent
- **Job:** Automates follow-ups, escalations, and reminders
- **What it does:** Watches your calls, visits, tickets, and SLAs. When something needs attention, it acts — not just alerts.
- **Output:** Automated workflows for repetitive operational tasks

### 4. Tech Agent
- **Job:** Talks to your tech stack and orchestrates automations
- **What it does:** Connects APIs, triggers webhooks, manages integrations. The glue between your systems.
- **Output:** Automated cross-system workflows without manual coding

### 5. Strategy Agent
- **Job:** Turns data into strategic insights and playbooks
- **What it does:** Generates weekly strategy reports, identifies growth opportunities, recommends next actions.
- **Output:** Executive-ready insights without waiting for weekly review meetings

## Privacy

Infinity CRM is built with privacy at its core:

- **No PII data sharing required** — the middleware can work with summary data or anonymized data
- **Runs on your infrastructure** — the AI layer deploys on your servers
- **Pure automation mode** — some agents operate without seeing any customer data at all (just triggering actions)
