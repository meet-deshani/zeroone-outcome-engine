## What It Is

Professional thermal design and rating service for TEMA Shell & Tube Heat Exchangers, using HTRI Xchanger Suite 9.0 (licensed). We design new heat exchangers from scratch or optimize your existing ones.

**Certification:** HTRI Certified
**Standards:** Full TEMA compliance (R, C, B classes)

---

## Two Service Types

### 1. Design & Simulation (Building from Scratch)
For new equipment:
- Complete thermal design for new heat exchangers
- Iterate geometry for the most efficient shell & tube configuration
- Optimize baffle spacing, tube layout, shell types (E, F, G, H, J, K, X)
- Full vibration analysis to prevent tube damage

### 2. Rating & Revamp (Optimizing the Old)
For existing equipment:
- Performance validation for existing exchangers
- Analyze fouling impact and check safety margins
- Simulate new operating conditions
- Recommend modifications to improve performance

## Capabilities

### HTRI Xchanger Suite 9.0
- Licensed professional software (not student/trial version)
- Uses Xist module for rigorous thermal-hydraulic analysis
- Industry-standard for process engineering worldwide

### TEMA Standards Compliance
- Full compliance with TEMA R (refinery), C (chemical), B (general) classes
- Optimize: baffle spacing, tube layout patterns, shell types
- Mechanical considerations: tube sheets, baffles, tie rods, structural integrity

### Analysis Scope
- Heat transfer coefficients
- Pressure drop calculations (shell-side and tube-side)
- Vibration analysis (acoustic resonance, flow-induced vibration)
- Fouling factor sensitivity
- Multi-phase flow (condensation, boiling, two-phase)

---

## 3-Step Process

| Step | What Happens |
|------|-------------|
| **1. Input Analysis & Validation** | We validate your physical properties, fouling factors, pressure drops, and operating conditions |
| **2. Thermal Simulation (HTRI Xist)** | We run the simulation — optimize Rho-V², baffle cuts, check acoustic resonance |
| **3. Optimization & Deliverables** | We balance heat transfer vs. pumping cost and deliver the final package |
