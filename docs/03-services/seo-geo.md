## FAQs

**What is GEO?**
GEO stands for Generative Engine Optimization. It's like SEO, but for AI. When someone asks ChatGPT "best consulting firm for X," GEO makes sure your brand shows up in the answer.

**Do I still need SEO?**
Yes. Google still drives most traffic. Do both — SEO for Google, GEO for AI. They complement each other.

**How do you track AI mentions?**
We run your brand through multiple AI models (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) weekly and track what they say about you, how often they recommend you, and how that changes over time.

**What's an AI Presence Score?**
A 0-100 score measuring how visible and positively represented your brand is across AI platforms. We check recommendation frequency, accuracy of information, and sentiment.

**How long until results?**
SEO improvements: 3-6 months (Google is slow). GEO improvements: can see changes within weeks (AI models update faster).

**What if AI says wrong things about us?**
We identify inaccuracies and create corrective content that AI models will learn from. Over time, AI starts saying the right things.
