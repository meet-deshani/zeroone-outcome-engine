## Overview

ZeroOne deploys 12 specialized AI agents across four categories. Each agent has a specific job, defined inputs, and measurable outputs. Agents run autonomously and are powered by the AI Council's multi-model architecture.

---

## Category 1: Data Guardians (Revenue Protection)

These agents protect your revenue by finding hidden costs and keeping your data clean.

### Agent 1: Money You're Losing Detective
- **Purpose:** Finds hidden costs and lost revenue buried in your transaction logs
- **Inputs:** Transaction Logs, Inventory Data, Ad Spend
- **Outputs:** Ranked Revenue Alerts, Leakage Report, Corrective Actions
- **Impact:** Typically recovers 12%+ in previously invisible margin losses

### Agent 2: Auto-Fix Your Data Feeds
- **Purpose:** Keeps your dashboards full and correct, always
- **Inputs:** API Sources, Legacy Databases, Excel Sheets
- **Outputs:** Fresh Data 24/7, Zero-Downtime Alerts, Self-Healing Logs
- **Impact:** Eliminates manual data entry and broken dashboards

### Agent 3: The Board-Ready Dashboard
- **Purpose:** Executive-grade visibility without asking analysts
- **Inputs:** Metrics Catalog, KPI Definitions
- **Outputs:** Live Profit View, Investor Updates, Drill-down Capability
- **Impact:** Board-ready reports generated automatically, no analyst bottleneck

### Agent 4: Data Cleaner
- **Purpose:** Removes duplicates and fixes bad contact info
- **Inputs:** CRM Contacts, Email Lists
- **Outputs:** Clean Customer Golden Record, Bounce Reduction, Higher Deliverability
- **Impact:** Email deliverability jumps, duplicate contacts eliminated

- **Impact:** SLA breaches drop to near-zero

## How Agents Are Deployed

1. **Discovery** — We map your current processes and identify pain points
2. **Selection** — Choose the agents that match your biggest problems
3. **Configuration** — Customize inputs/outputs for your specific systems
4. **Deployment** — Deploy on your infrastructure (Private AI) or managed cloud
5. **Monitoring** — Agents run 24/7 with the AI Council handling failover
6. **Optimization** — Continuous improvement based on real results
