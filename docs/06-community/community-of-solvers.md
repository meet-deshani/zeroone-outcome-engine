## Mission

> **"Make AI private, free, and accessible."**

The Community of Solvers is ZeroOne's open ecosystem where builders, entrepreneurs, trainers, and learners come together to build Private AI solutions for real-world problems.

---

## Six Community Roles

### 1. Founder
**Who:** Meet Deshani
**Mission:** Making AI private, free for all, cutting internet dependency
**Role:** Sets the vision, architects core systems, mentors the community

### 2. AI Engineers
**Who:** Core technical builders
**Mission:** Building the models and infrastructure that power Private AI
**Role:** Develop production systems, maintain AI Council, create new models, review community contributions

### 3. Vibe Coders
**Who:** Builder community members
**Mission:** Building wrappers, tools, and applications using Private AI
**Role:** Create real-world tools, test in production scenarios, share workflows, mentor learners
**What makes them unique:** They're builders who "vibe" with the technology — moving fast, shipping code, testing ideas

### 4. Entrepreneurs & Supporters
**Who:** Business founders, investors, and champions
**Mission:** Funding, backing, and championing the Private AI movement
**Role:** Provide resources, connect builders with clients, fund bounties, spread the word

### 5. Trainers
**Who:** AI educators and workshop leaders
**Mission:** Teaching the next generation of AI builders
**Role:** Conduct workshops, create training materials, mentor learners through curriculum, develop best practices

### 6. Learners
**Who:** New members starting their AI journey
**Mission:** Getting hands-on with Private AI
**Role:** Complete training modules, build first AI agents, contribute to bounties, grow into Vibe Coders or Engineers

## How to Join

1. **Visit** the Community page at [zeroonedotsai.consulting/community](https://zeroonedotsai.consulting/community)
2. **Pick your role** — choose the role that best describes you
3. **Start building** — join a bounty, attend a workshop, or start the training sprint
4. **Grow** — advance from Learner → Vibe Coder → AI Engineer as your skills develop

## Community Values

1. **Build, don't talk.** Working code > theoretical discussions
2. **Share what works.** Workflows, templates, and lessons — open by default
3. **Own your work.** Everything you build is yours to keep, patent, or sell
4. **Help others grow.** Mentor the next generation of builders
5. **Privacy first.** Every solution respects data ownership and privacy
