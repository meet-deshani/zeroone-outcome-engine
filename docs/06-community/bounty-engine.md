# Bounty Engine

> **Access:** COMMUNITY

---

## What It Is

The Bounty Engine is ZeroOne's marketplace where anyone can post a problem, set a price, and let the community solve it. Think of it as a freelance platform specifically for AI solutions — but you keep all the IP.

**Tagline:** "Got a Problem? Post a Bounty."

---

## How It Works (3 Steps)

### Step 1: POST
- Describe your problem clearly
- Link a Google Doc with details, data samples, and requirements
- Name your price (the bounty)
- Submit through the website form

### Step 2: SOLVE
- Community members review posted bounties
- Builders (Vibe Coders, AI Engineers) pick bounties that match their skills
- They build solutions using Private AI tools
- Solutions are tested and validated before submission

### Step 3: PAY
- You review the submitted solution
- If it meets your requirements, you approve and release payment
- You keep 100% of the IP — the solution is yours
- Builder gets paid, builds reputation

---

## Rules

### For Bounty Posters
- **Clear requirements** — the more specific your Google Doc, the better solutions you'll get
- **Fair pricing** — set bounties that reflect the complexity of the problem
- **Timely review** — review submissions within the agreed timeframe
- **Honest feedback** — if a solution doesn't meet requirements, explain why

### For Bounty Solvers
- **Quality work** — solutions should be production-ready, not prototypes
- **Private AI first** — use ZeroOne's Private AI stack where possible
- **IP transfer** — all work product belongs to the bounty poster
- **Communication** — ask clarifying questions before building, not after

---

## IP Ownership

This is the critical rule:

> **The bounty poster owns 100% of the IP.**

When you post a bounty and pay for a solution:
- The code is yours
- The model is yours
- The data stays yours
- You can patent it
- You can publish research about it
- You can sell it
- You can modify it

The solver retains no rights to the work product after payment.

## What Makes a Good Bounty

| Element | Example |
|---------|---------|
| **Clear problem statement** | "I need an AI that reads invoices and extracts line items" |
| **Google Doc with details** | Sample invoices, expected output format, accuracy requirements |
| **Defined scope** | "Process 100 invoices per day, 95% accuracy on line items" |
| **Fair price** | Based on complexity: ₹5k for simple automation, ₹50k+ for complex AI systems |
| **Timeline** | "Need solution within 2 weeks" |

## How to Post a Bounty

1. Go to [zeroonedotsai.consulting/community](https://zeroonedotsai.consulting/community)
2. Scroll to the Bounty Engine section
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Problem Title** — short, descriptive name
   - **Google Doc Link** — detailed requirements, samples, specs
   - **Budget / Price** — what you're willing to pay
   - **Contact Email** — how solvers can reach you
4. Submit — the bounty goes live for the community to see


## FAQ

**Who can post bounties?**
Anyone — businesses, individuals, students, other communities.

**Who can solve bounties?**
Anyone in the Community of Solvers — Vibe Coders, AI Engineers, Learners (with mentor oversight).

**What if I'm not happy with the solution?**
You can reject with feedback. The solver can revise. If it still doesn't meet requirements, you don't pay.

**Can I post anonymous bounties?**
You can keep your company name private, but you need to provide contact details for the solver to ask clarifying questions.

**Is there a minimum bounty amount?**
No minimum, but realistic pricing attracts better builders. Complex AI work typically starts at ₹10,000+.
