## What It Is

The Student Challenge is ZeroOne's program for students who want to learn AI by building real products. The best performers get mentorship, real client work, and a path to earning ₹1 Lakh/month.

This isn't a course — it's a launchpad.

---

## The Prize

> **Best idea wins 1 Month of Free Mentorship.**

Submit your AI idea. The most promising idea gets 1:1 mentorship from ZeroOne architects to turn it into a working product.

---

## The Career Path

```
Submit Idea → Win Mentorship → Build Agent → Get Clients → Earn ₹1 Lakh/Month
```

### Step 1: Submit Your Idea
Come up with an AI solution for a real problem. Write it up. Submit it through the community page.

### Step 2: Win Mentorship
The best ideas get selected for 1-month free mentorship. You work 1:1 with ZeroOne architects who help you design and build.

### Step 3: Build AI Agents
Learn to build production-ready AI agents. Understand ethics, security, privacy, and quality standards. Ship real code.

### Step 4: Get Clients
Top performers get connected to real clients through ZeroOne's network. You build AI solutions for paying businesses.

### Step 5: Earn ₹1 Lakh/Month
Full-time roles for top performers. Build AI tools for ZeroOne's client base. The billing model:
- **You keep 90%** of client billing
- **Pay 10%** for infrastructure usage
- **No cap on earnings** — the more clients you serve, the more you earn
